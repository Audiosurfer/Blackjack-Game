Blackjack App

How To Play

  • This app is based on the card game Blackjack.
  • Click "Yes" to draw a card, which will add a number from 1-11 to your total.
  • When you want to stop, click "No" and it will compare your score with the computer's to select a winner.
  • If you get exactly 21, you win automatically!
  • Watch out though, as going over 21 results in an automatic loss.
  • To hide these directions, click on Directions again.
